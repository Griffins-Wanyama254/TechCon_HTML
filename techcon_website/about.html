History of TechCon

TechCon began in 2010 as a small developer meet-up and has since grown into a leading international technology conference. Over the years, it has hosted thousands of attendees and featured cutting-edge innovations from around the world.

Our Mission

TechCon exists to foster inclusive innovation, inspire collaboration, and empower future generations through technology. We aim to connect communities and drive positive change by making tech accessible to all.
